# Privacy Policy — DaysInDots **Effective date:** 2026-01-15 DaysInDots is a local-first iOS app for tracking dates and visualizing time using dots, including Home Screen widgets. This Privacy Policy explains what information is used and how. --- ## 1) Summary - DaysInDots **does not collect, transmit, sell, or share** your personal data to any server operated by the developer. - Your data is stored **locally on your device** (and inside the app’s widget extension via App Groups). - Optional iOS permissions (Calendar, Notifications) are used only for features you enable. --- ## 2) Data the app stores (on-device) DaysInDots may store the following information locally: - **Events** you create or import (title, date, color, notes, widget flag). - **Settings** (selected mode, language, widget appearance, etc.). - **Reminder settings** for events (if you enable reminders). This data is stored using Apple’s on-device storage (e.g., `UserDefaults`) and an **App Group** container to allow widgets to read the same data. --- ## 3) Calendar access (optional) If you choose to import events from Apple Calendar, the app may request Calendar permission. The app uses this access only to read calendar events for import. Imported events are stored locally in DaysInDots. You can revoke Calendar access at any time in iOS Settings. --- ## 4) Notifications (optional) If you enable reminders, the app may request permission to send notifications. Reminders are scheduled as **local notifications** on your device. You can change notification permissions in iOS Settings. --- ## 5) Analytics, tracking, third‑party SDKs - DaysInDots does **not** include third‑party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs. - DaysInDots does **not** use cookies or web tracking. Apple may collect certain diagnostic or usage information as part of iOS. The developer does not receive this information unless you explicitly share it through Apple mechanisms. --- ## 6) Data sharing DaysInDots does not share your app data with third parties. The only “sharing” is local: the widget extension reads the same on-device storage via App Groups. --- ## 7) Data retention & deletion You control your data: - Use the in‑app option **Delete all events** to remove stored events. - Uninstalling the app removes the app’s local storage from the device. - If you use iCloud device backups, your app data may be included in your backup according to Apple’s backup settings. --- ## 8) Children’s privacy DaysInDots is not intended to collect information from children, and it does not knowingly collect personal information. --- ## 9) Changes to this policy This policy may be updated from time to time.
